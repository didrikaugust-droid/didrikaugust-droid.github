Gdansk, Poland

Facts about Gdansk

  • Language: Polish
  • Currency: Polish zloty
  • Population: About 470,000

About Gdansk

Gdansk is a historic city in northern Poland on the Baltic Sea.

The city is famous for its colorful buildings and long maritime history.

Famous places

  • Long Market
  • Neptune Fountain
  • St. Mary's Church
  • Gdansk Old Town

My trip

I walked around the old town and saw many beautiful buildings.

Gdansk was a very interesting city with a lot of history.
